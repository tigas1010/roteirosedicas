Douro Valley Private Tour

Explore Portugal’s oldest demarcated wine region, a UNESCO World Heritage site. Visit traditional wineries, taste Port and DOC wines, enjoy a rabelo boat cruise and a regional lunch with breathtaking views.

What’s Included

  • Visits to wineries with guided tastings of Port and regional wines
  • Traditional rabelo boat cruise on the Douro River
  • Regional lunch (included)
  • Stops at scenic viewpoints for photos
  • Insights into the history and production of Port wine

Detailed Itinerary

09:00 - Pick-up in Porto

Meet your guide at your hotel/accommodation

10:30 - First Winery Visit

Vineyard and cellar tour with wine tasting

12:30 - Regional Lunch

Typical meal at a local restaurant (included)

14:30 - Boat Cruise

One-hour rabelo boat cruise on the Douro River

15:30 - Viewpoints

Photo stops at the most spectacular landscapes

18:00 - Return to Porto

Drop-off at your hotel/accommodation in Porto
