Fátima & Aveiro Tour

Combine spirituality and tradition: visit the Sanctuary of Fátima and discover Aveiro, the “Portuguese Venice”, with a moliceiro boat ride and local sweets.

What’s Included

  • Fátima Sanctuary: Basilica and Chapel of the Apparitions
  • Moliceiro boat ride through Aveiro’s canals
  • Tasting of “ovos moles” (optional)
  • Costa Nova striped houses and coastal stroll
  • Expert guide and private transportation
Book Now

Moliceiro cruise and historic center

Striped houses and photo time
