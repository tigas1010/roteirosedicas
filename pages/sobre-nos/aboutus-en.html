About Us

Discover the story of Roteiros&Dicas, our values, mission and team. We are passionate about Portugal and love to create authentic experiences for all our customers!

Who We Are

Founded by Portugal lovers, Roteiros&Dicas was born from the desire to show the best of our country to those who visit us. Our team consists of local guides, knowledgeable about the traditions, stories and secrets of each region.

We value human connection, authenticity and care in every detail. Our goal is to provide true experiences, tailored to each customer's profile.

We believe traveling means discovering, feeling, tasting and taking memories for life. With us, every itinerary is unique!

Mission, Vision & Values

  • Mission: Share the best of Portugal with authenticity and passion.
  • Vision: Be a reference in personalized tourism and memorable experiences.
  • Values: Honesty, closeness, dedication, quality and respect for local culture.
